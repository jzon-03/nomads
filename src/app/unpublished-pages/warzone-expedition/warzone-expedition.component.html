Season 1 Warzone Expedition

Basic Rules

  • We will be able to place “Alliance Outposts” in other Warzones.
  • Once the outpost is placed, it cannot be removed. Starting from the outpost, we can capture Military Strongholds in that Warzone within range.
  • The limit of capturing 2 Military Strongholds per day applies across all Warzones. You can capture only 2 per day, regardless of where they are placed.
  • Next Outpost unlocks every week. We can have a maximum of 4 outposts.
  • It is possible to place a maximum of 1 outpost per Warzone (Server).
  • The outposts do not need to be connected to the target.
    • Military Strongholds need to be connected to what is going to be captured/attacked.
  • You can capture any level of city on other Warzones:
    • You just need to be connected through a Military Stronghold.
    • No need to capture a level 1 city to capture a level 2 city.
    • Plan the capture of Military Strongholds accordingly to this rule.
  • When Outposts are placed, they have a protection status:
    • They cannot be attacked until they are built.
    • The more reinforcement placed, the faster the construction.
  • Once an outpost is built:
    • It can be attacked with single attacks (no rallies).
    • It is possible to rebuild destroyed outposts.
  • If your outpost gets destroyed:
    • You can rebuild it.
    • The outpost is needed only to capture the first Military Stronghold in another server.
    • Based on its placement, you can capture adjacent Military Strongholds.
    • It is otherwise useless.
